## Award-Winning Final Year Project Presentation

Team Aero-Sense from the University of Ruhuna secured **3rd place (All-Island)** at the Undergraduate Thesis Project Competition 2024, organized by the IEEE Signal Processing Society Chapter Sri Lanka in collaboration with SLTC Centre of Telecommunication Research (CTR) [page:2]. This competition brought together Sri Lanka's brightest minds in Signal Processing [page:2].

## Project Overview

The project focused on "Joint Communication and Sensing at ELF using AI-Enabled Radio Receivers," developed as a final-year project in the Department of Electrical and Information Engineering at the University of Ruhuna [page:2].
