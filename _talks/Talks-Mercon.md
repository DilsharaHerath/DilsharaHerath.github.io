I presented my research paper at the **11th Moratuwa Engineering Research Conference (MERCon 2025)**, held on **August 14-15, 2025** at the University of Moratuwa, Sri Lanka.

## Paper Details

**Track:** Computer Vision and Image Processing  
**Presentation Date:** August 14 or 15, 2025 
**Paper Title:** Unveiling Misalignment Fault Severities: A Novel SCD-CNN Framework for Rotating Machinery


The paper was published in IEEE Xplore and is available [here](https://ieeexplore.ieee.org/document/11217055).

MERCon is the premier engineering research conference in Sri Lanka, organized by the University of Moratuwa. The 2025 edition was held as a hybrid event with technical co-sponsorship from IEEE Sri Lanka Section.
