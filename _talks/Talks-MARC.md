As a Research Assistant at the Multidisciplinary AI Research Center (MARC), University of Peradeniya, I volunteered at three AI outreach workshops in 2025, supporting professors and research assistants in delivering impactful sessions to students across Sri Lanka.

## Session 1: Vavuniya - Meet AI Workshop

**Date:** September 2, 2025 | **Venue:** University of Vavuniya | **Audience:** Undergraduates  

**#MARCGoesNorth Part 2** - Workshop titled *"Meet AI: Tools for Exploratory Learning"* introduced undergraduates to AI research and project-based learning methodologies through interactive sessions and practical demonstrations.

## Session 2: Hardy Institute - AI Awareness

**Date:** June 2, 2025 | **Venue:** Hardy Advanced Technological Institute, Ampara | **Audience:** ~500 A/L students  

AI awareness session for rural Advanced Level students from 8 schools in Ampara district, exploring AI's potential to transform education, healthcare, and agriculture.

## About MARC Outreach

These sessions were part of MARC's mission to democratize AI education across Sri Lanka. As a Research Assistant, I supported workshop delivery, participant engagement, technical demonstrations, and logistics coordination.
