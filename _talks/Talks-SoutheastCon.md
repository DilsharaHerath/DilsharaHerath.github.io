## Conference Highlights

The conference brought together over 800 onsite participants and numerous virtual attendees from around the world to discuss cutting-edge research in engineering and technology.

The AI and Predictive Modeling track showcased innovative research in machine learning, artificial intelligence applications, and data-driven predictive systems, aligning with the conference's focus on advancing technological solutions across multiple engineering disciplines.

## About IEEE SoutheastCon

IEEE SoutheastCon is an annual conference organized by IEEE Region 3, serving as a premier platform for presenting research, networking with professionals, and participating in technical competitions. The 2024 edition was hosted at The Westin Peachtree Plaza in Atlanta, with hybrid format enabling both physical and virtual participation.

**Conference Website:** [attend.ieee.org/southeastcon-2024](https://attend.ieee.org/southeastcon-2024/)
